About Me.

I'm Manas, a developer, and innovator with a passion for technology and teaching. I am currently pursuing my MS degree in the Computer Science department at Texas State University. I have helped many students and colleagues with programming and other life skills to be imaginative and independent developers.

My first interaction with programming was when I was looping soundtracks as a 15-year-old and wanted to build a metronome by myself. Since then, I have created multiple websites, apps, and interfaces. I soon realized that I am passionate about designing products from end to end. And therefore, I spend most of my time researching products and collaborating with experts from different fields to combine our knowledge and create magic.

To quote the genius physicist, A Einstein, "creativity is intelligence having fun." And, to add to it, I supplement "fun is innovation in recursion."
